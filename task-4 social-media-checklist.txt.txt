PART B - SOCIAL MEDIA CHECKLIST
? Do's
- Maintain a professional and respectful profile.
- Think before posting any content online.
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
- Verify information before sharing or reposting.
- Use privacy settings to control your personal information visibility.

? Don'ts
- Do not share sensitive personal information (phone number, address, OTPs).
- Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.
- Do not click on suspicious or unknown links.
- Avoid oversharing personal life details publicly.
- Do not engage in online arguments, trolling, or cyberbullying.
